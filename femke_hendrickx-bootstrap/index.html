What is Drum and Bass?

Drum and bass is a genre of electronic music characterized by fast breakbeats, heavy basslines, and intricate rhythms.

History of Drum and Bass

Drum and bass originated in the early 1990s in the UK as a fusion of breakbeat, jungle, and bass music. It quickly gained popularity and has since evolved into various sub-genres.
